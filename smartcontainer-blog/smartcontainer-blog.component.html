Revolutionizing Shipment Security with Inbuilt GPS

Safeobuddy’s Smart Lock comes equipped with an inbuilt GPS, enabling real-time tracking of your containers. This feature allows businesses to monitor their shipments’ exact locations, providing peace of mind and improving overall supply chain transparency. With real-time GPS tracking, you can stay updated on the status of your goods, ensuring they are on the right path to their destination.

Convenient Locking and Unlocking Mechanisms

One of the standout features of Safeobuddy’s Smart Lock is its ability to function without a network. This means you can lock and unlock the container even in areas with poor or no network coverage, ensuring uninterrupted operations. The lock can be conveniently unlocked using a mobile phone, adding a layer of flexibility and ease. Additionally, it offers remote unlocking through a web dashboard, allowing authorized personnel to manage container access from anywhere in the world.

  • Enhanced Security with Invisible and Keyless Design The Smart Lock’s design is both innovative and practical. It is invisible from the outside, reducing the risk of tampering or break-ins. The keyless lock mechanism eliminates the need for physical keys, which can be lost or duplicated, enhancing security further. Safeobuddy’s Smart Lock also includes unauthorized lock open alerts, notifying you instantly if someone tries to tamper with the container, thereby providing an extra layer of security.

  • Battery Backup and Authorized Driver Access: To ensure continuous operation, the Smart Lock comes with an inbuilt battery backup. This feature guarantees that the lock remains functional even during power outages or in remote areas without reliable power sources. Moreover, the lock allows for authorized driver access, meaning only designated personnel can open the container, further safeguarding your shipment from unauthorized access.

  • Real-Time Status Updates:Safeobuddy’s Smart Lock offers real-time status updates of the lock’s open and close activities. This feature is crucial for maintaining an accurate record of who accessed the container and when. It provides valuable data for audit trails, ensuring accountability and transparency in your logistics operations.
